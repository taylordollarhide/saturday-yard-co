From a homeowner in Erie, CO

”My front yard burns out every July no matter how much I water. What am I missing?”
Nine times out of ten it's sprinkler coverage, not schedule. Before adjusting your timer, walk your yard while the system runs and look for dry gaps between heads. Fix the coverage first — then dial in the timing.
